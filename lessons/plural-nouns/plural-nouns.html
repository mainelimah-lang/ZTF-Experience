Plural Nouns

Cover

Number14
Plural nouns are words that represent more than one person, place, thing, or idea.
  • 👉 Most nouns form the plural by simply adding “-s”.
    📂 project → projects

    🧑‍💼 client → clients

    💡 idea → ideas

    🕒 meeting → meetings

    📄 document → documents

    🧒 Employee → Employees

⚠️ However, not all nouns follow this simple rule. Let’s take a closer look at the different rules for making countable nouns plural. 👀🔢

  • Regular Plurals

    • Nouns ending in consonant + y

      👉 Remove “y” and add "-ies"

      🌆 City → Cities

      🇧🇷 Country → Countries

      🏢 Company → Companies

      📊 Strategy → Strategies

      ⏩ Priority → Priorities

      🎯 Opportunity → Opportunities

    • Nouns ending in -s, -x, -z, -ch, -sh

      👉 Add "-es"

      🧾 Tax → Taxes

      ⌚ Watch - Watches

      📚 Class → Classes

      📥 Inbox → Inboxes

      📈 Business → Businesses

      💼 Branch → Branches

    • Nouns ending in -f or -fe

      👉 Remove “f” or “fe” and add "-ves"

      📚 Shelf → Shelves

      🔐 Thief → Thieves

      🔪 Knife → Knives

      🧬 Life - Lives

      🐺 Wolf → Wolves

      🍃 Leaf - Leaves

      ⚠️ Some words that end in "f" or “fe” form the plural by simply adding an "s”.


      🤝 Chief → Chiefs

      🙏 Belief → Beliefs

      🧗‍♂️ Cliff → Cliffs

      💬 Proof → Proofs

      🏠 Roof → Roofs

      🦒 Giraffe → Giraffes

    • Nouns ending in consonant + o

      👉 Add "-es"

      🦸‍♂️ Hero → Heroes

      🥔 Potato → Potatoes

      🍅 Tomato → Tomatoes

      🌋 Volcano → Volcanoes

      🌪️ Tornado → Tornadoes

      🔊 Echo → Echoes

      ⚠️ Some words that end in "o" form the plural by simply adding an "s”.

      📷 Photo → Photos

      🎹 Piano → Pianos

      🎬 Studio → Studios

      📺 Video → Videos

      📻 Radio → Radios

      ⚖️ Kilo → Kilos

  • Irregular Plurals

    • 🚫 No Rules

      💡Some nouns don’t follow specific rules. They must be memorized.

      🦶 Foot → Feet 👣

      🦷 Tooth → Teeth 🦷🦷

      🎲 Die → Dice 🎲🎲

      🐭 Mouse → Mice 🐭🐭

      🐂 Ox → Oxen 🐂🐂

      👩 Woman → Women 👩👩

      👨 Man → Men 👨👨

      🧍 Person → People 🧍🧍

    • ❗No change

      💡 Some nouns stay the same in the plural.

      🐟 Fish → Fish 🐟🐟

      🐑 Sheep → Sheep 🐑🐑

      🦌 Deer → Deer 🦌🦌

      ✂️ Scissors → Scissors ✂️✂️

      🧬 Species → Species 🧬🧬

      📺 Series - Series 📺📺

    • 🌐 Latin & Greek Origin Plurals

      1️⃣ Nouns ending in -is → change to -es

      • Basis → Bases
      • Crisis → Crises
      • Analysis → Analyses

      2️⃣ Nouns ending in -on → change to -a

      • Criterion → Criteria
      • Phenomenon → Phenomena

      3️⃣ Nouns ending in -us → change to -i

      • Cactus → Cacti 🌵
      • Focus → Foci

      💡 Not all nouns follow these patterns!

      Some take regular plurals:

      • Bonus → Bonuses
      • Virus → Viruses
