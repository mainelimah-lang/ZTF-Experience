‚åõ Time Expressions

The Past Simple is often used with a time expression to show when the action happened. These expressions help give context and make the meaning more specific.

üìÜ Specific time references

  • yesterday
  • last (night, week, month, year, summer, etc.)
  • ago (two days ago, many years ago)
  • in (a specific year, month, or part of the day)
  • on (a specific day or date)
  • at (a specific time)
  • this morning / this afternoon / this evening
