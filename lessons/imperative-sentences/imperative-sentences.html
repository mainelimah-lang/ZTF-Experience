💡 Imperative sentences are used to give commands, instructions, or requests.

🔎 Types of Imperative Sentences

📣 Positive Imperatives

Encourage action or give direct instructions.

→ “Finish your meal.”

🚫 Negative Imperatives

Instruct someone not to do something.

→ “Don’t run in the hall.”

😱 Exclamatory Imperatives

Express urgency or strong emotion. Often end with an exclamation mark.

→ “Watch out!”

✨ Politeness in Imperatives

You can make imperatives more polite by adding the word “please”:
At the beginning: “Please close the window.”At the end: “Close the window, please.”
